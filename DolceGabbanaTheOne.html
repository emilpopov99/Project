Composition of the fragrance

Firstly, you will feel the notes of grapefruit, coriander and basil coming into the spotlight, which will ensure an elegant and radiant first impression, unveiling an oriental, spicy composition. As soon as the introductory notes step back, the spicy combination of cardamom and ginger will come through, accompanied by the sweetness of orange blossom. The last phase is composed of tobacco, which gives the fragrance its richness, as well as amber and cedar, which ensure a long-lasting lingering reverberation.

Story of the Fragrance

It's not only the fragrance itself but also the flacon of the Dolce & Gabbana The One for Men that makes it extraordinary. The design stays true to The One collection and offers a clean, elegant geometric bottle in dark shades. On the very top sits a shiny, metal lid.
